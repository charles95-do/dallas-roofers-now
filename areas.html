Where We Work

Proudly serving homeowners and businesses across Dallas and the DFW Metroplex.

Dallas & North Dallas

Our home base. We serve every neighborhood in Dallas and the northern suburbs.

Dallas

Our home city and the heart of everything we do. From North Dallas to Oak Cliff, Lake Highlands to Lakewood — we know Dallas neighborhoods inside and out. Hail season hits this city hard, and we're always ready to respond.

Richardson

A thriving city just north of Dallas with a mix of established homes and new construction. Richardson's mature tree canopy means storm debris and shingle damage are common after severe weather.

Garland

With a large inventory of homes built in the 1970s–90s, Garland is one of our busiest service areas for roof replacements and storm damage restoration. We keep crews close to respond fast.

Mesquite

Mesquite homeowners deal with the same severe storms that batter the rest of DFW. We provide quick emergency response and full roof replacements for this hardworking east Dallas suburb.

Rowlett

Rowlett knows severe weather — the 2015 tornado put this community on the map. We've been helping Rowlett homeowners protect and restore their roofs ever since, and we're proud to serve this resilient community.

Sachse

A growing suburb between Garland and Wylie with beautiful newer homes. We handle everything from hail damage inspections to full replacements for Sachse families.

Wylie

One of the fastest-growing cities in Collin County. Wylie's rapid development means plenty of new construction roofing as well as storm damage repairs for established neighborhoods.

Murphy

A small, family-friendly city with well-kept homes that deserve quality roofing. We provide inspections, repairs, and full replacements to keep Murphy roofs in top shape year-round.

Plano

One of DFW's largest suburbs with a huge range of homes — from starter houses to luxury estates. Plano gets hammered by hail nearly every spring, making us one of the most-called roofers in the area.

Allen

A fast-growing Collin County city with beautiful subdivisions. Allen homeowners trust us for everything from post-storm inspections to insurance claim assistance and full roof replacements.

McKinney

Historic downtown charm meets booming new development. McKinney's mix of older homes and new construction keeps our crews busy with repairs, replacements, and inspections alike.

Frisco

One of the fastest-growing cities in America. Frisco's newer homes need storm damage repair just as much as older ones — Texas hail doesn't discriminate. We're a trusted name in Frisco roofing.

Irving

From the Las Colinas business district to Valley Ranch neighborhoods, Irving has a diverse mix of residential and commercial roofing needs. We handle both with the same level of professionalism.

Grand Prairie

Sitting between Dallas and Fort Worth, Grand Prairie homeowners get the same severe weather as the rest of DFW. We provide fast response and quality workmanship for this mid-cities community.

Arlington

The entertainment capital of DFW is also home to thousands of families who need reliable roofing. From emergency repairs to full replacements, Arlington homeowners count on us when storms roll through.
