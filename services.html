Our Roofing Services

Everything your roof needs — from emergency hail damage repair to a full tear-off and replacement.

Emergency Roof Repair

When a Texas storm tears through your neighborhood, you can't wait days for a roofer to show up. We offer same-day emergency response across Dallas and the DFW Metroplex. Whether it's an active leak, blown-off shingles, or a tree limb through your roof — we get there fast and secure your home.

  • Same-day emergency response (including nights and weekends)
  • Emergency tarping and board-up to prevent further damage
  • Missing or damaged shingle replacement
  • Flashing repair around chimneys, vents, and skylights
  • Pipe boot and vent seal replacement
  • Interior water damage assessment

Roof Replacement

When repairs aren't enough, we do full tear-off and replacement with materials built to handle the brutal Texas sun and severe storm season. Most residential roofs completed in 1-3 days with minimal disruption to your household.

  • Full tear-off and disposal of old roofing
  • Architectural (dimensional) shingle installation
  • Impact-resistant shingles (Class 4 hail-rated)
  • Metal roofing (standing seam, corrugated)
  • Synthetic underlayment and ice/water shield
  • New drip edge, ridge vent, and flashing
  • Manufacturer warranty + workmanship guarantee

Hail & Wind Damage

North Texas is one of the most hail-prone regions in the country. Every spring, severe storms pound the DFW area with golf ball-sized hail and 70+ mph winds. We handle the full restoration process — from emergency tarping to insurance claims to the final shingle.

  • Emergency tarping and temporary waterproofing
  • Comprehensive hail and wind damage assessment
  • Insurance claim assistance (we meet with your adjuster on-site)
  • Full documentation with photos for your claim
  • Tree and debris removal from roof
  • Complete storm damage restoration

Flat Roof / Commercial Roofing

We service flat roofs and low-slope commercial buildings across the DFW Metroplex. From strip malls to office parks to warehouses — our commercial team delivers reliable roofing solutions with minimal disruption to your business.

  • TPO single-ply membrane roofing
  • EPDM rubber roofing
  • Modified bitumen roofing
  • Commercial roof repair and patching
  • Preventive maintenance contracts
  • Roof coating and restoration

Roof Inspection & Maintenance

Buying a home? Filing an insurance claim? Want to check on your roof after storm season? We provide thorough inspections with a detailed written report and honest recommendations — we won't sell you a roof you don't need.

  • Pre-purchase home buyer inspections
  • Post-storm damage inspections
  • Insurance claim inspections
  • Annual maintenance inspections
  • Detailed report with photos and recommendations
  • Preventive maintenance plans

Gutter Installation

Texas thunderstorms dump inches of rain in minutes. Without proper gutters, that water hammers your foundation, floods your flower beds, and erodes your landscaping. We install complete gutter systems designed for heavy North Texas rainfall.

  • Seamless aluminum gutter installation
  • Gutter guard/leaf protection systems
  • Downspout installation and rerouting
  • Gutter repair and reattachment
  • Soffit and fascia repair/replacement
  • Custom color matching to your home's exterior
